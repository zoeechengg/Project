Welcome to Hong Kong Foodies! Get started on your culinary journey in Hong Kong.

Hong Kong is the culinary center of Asia, offering cuisines from all over the world. Every tourist who comes to Hong Kong must have the essential Hong Kong foods - traditional dim sum, seafood and Chinese barbeque meats. However, apart from the local food in Hong Kong, the city offers so many other varities that you will never get bored. There are hundreds of restaurants that offer international cuisines where people can enjoy the dishes of India, Thailand, Italy, France, to name a few.
