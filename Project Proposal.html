About Website

I have always loved trying new types of food and disovering the best restaurants wherever I go. In a city like Hong Kong, where I grew up, there are always new restaurants constantly opening up. This website is going to be based on my past experiences dining in Hong Kong and what I think are the best places to go to.

Objective: This would be an informative website for Hong Kong residents or travlers planning on visiting Hong Kong in helping them decide or offering recommendations as to where they should go for good food.

Target Audience: (1) Hong Kong residents looking for new restaurants to try. (2) Tourists planning on coming to Hong Kong.

Website Content

  • Homepage - About Website + an introduction into Hong Kong food culture
  • Hong Kong Local/Street Food
  • Asian Cuisines (Japanese, Thai, Indian)
  • Western Restaurants (American, French, Spanish)
  • Latin American Restaurants (Brazilian, Mexican)
